    About us

    Our home automation system offers you the convenience of controlling your lights, temperature, and water from anywhere using just your smartphone or tablet. You can also remotely lock your house or garage doors, make coffee, clean your house, change music, and manage your calendar. All of this is available for only $5 a month, giving you an affordable and hassle-free way to manage your home.
